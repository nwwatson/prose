# Prose

**A personal newsletter and publishing platform for independent writers**

Prose empowers writers, journalists, and creators to build audiences, publish content, and monetize their work—all from a single, elegant platform. Whether you're starting a newsletter, launching a paid publication, or building a community around your writing, Prose provides the tools you need without the complexity.

---

## Why Prose?

The internet has transformed how writers connect with readers. No longer do you need a publisher, an editor, or a media company to reach your audience. Prose puts you in control—your content, your audience, your revenue.

### For Writers
- Focus on writing with a clean, distraction-free editor
- Own your subscriber list and reader relationships
- Monetize on your terms with flexible subscription options

### For Readers

- Direct connection to writers you love

## Core Features

### Publishing & Writing

**Distraction-Free Editor**
Write in a clean, minimal environment powered by Lexxy (Basecamp's modern rich text editor built on Meta's Lexical framework). The editor gets out of your way so you can focus on what matters—your words. Features include real-time Markdown support, code syntax highlighting, @mentions, and seamless Action Text integration.

**Rich Content Support**
- Text formatting with full Markdown support
- Image uploads with automatic optimization
- Audio and video embeds
- Content embeds from Twitter/X, YouTube, Spotify, CodePen, and GitHub Gists
- Syntax highlighting for code snippets
- Footnotes and sidenotes for academic and long-form writing

**Post Templates**
Create reusable templates for recurring content like weekly roundups, Q&As, or interview formats. Save time and maintain consistency across your publication.

**Revision History**
Every change is tracked. Browse previous versions, compare differences, and restore earlier drafts with a single click. Never lose your work again.

**Co-Authoring**
Collaborate with other writers on posts. Add multiple authors with proper attribution and role designations (primary author, contributor, editor).

**Scheduling**
Write when inspiration strikes, publish when your audience is ready. Schedule posts in advance and Prose handles the rest.

**Series & Collections**
Organize related posts into ordered series. Perfect for serialized stories, multi-part tutorials, or ongoing investigations.

**Tags & Categories**
Fine-grained organization beyond sections. Tag posts for easy discovery and filtering.

**Pinned Posts**
Feature your most important content at the top of your publication. Highlight introductions, key resources, or evergreen content.

### Email Delivery

**Direct to Inbox**
Every post goes directly to your subscribers' inboxes via Mailgun's reliable email delivery service. No algorithms, no feeds—just your words in front of your readers. Development uses Letter Opener for safe email testing.

**Preview Text Customization**
Control the snippet that appears in email clients. Craft compelling previews that drive opens.

**Plain Text Editor**
Customize the plain text version of your emails for maximum compatibility and accessibility.

**Resend to Non-Openers**
Didn't get the opens you expected? Resend to subscribers who missed it with a different subject line.

**Send Time Optimization**
ML-powered analysis of your subscribers' reading habits. Send emails when each subscriber is most likely to open.

**Email Preferences**
Let subscribers choose how they receive your content:
- Immediate delivery
- Daily digest
- Weekly digest
- Per-section preferences

---

### Subscriber Management

**Subscriber Dashboard**
View, search, and manage your entire audience from one place. See engagement scores, subscription status, and activity history.

**Subscriber Tags**
Organize readers by interests, behavior, or any criteria you choose. Use tags for targeted sends and personalized content.

**Engagement Scoring**
Understand who your most engaged readers are. Track open rates, click rates, and reading behavior per subscriber.

**Subscriber Notes**
Keep private notes about individual subscribers. Remember context from conversations, track preferences, or note special circumstances.

**Bulk Actions**
Efficiently manage your list at scale:
- Bulk tag or untag subscribers
- Export segments to CSV
- Remove inactive subscribers

**Suppression Lists**
Automatically manage unsubscribes, bounces, and complaints. Keep your list healthy and your deliverability high.

**Cohort Analysis**
Understand subscriber retention over time. See how different cohorts behave and identify what drives long-term engagement.

### Monetization

**Paid Subscriptions**
Offer monthly or yearly paid subscriptions. Set your own prices and keep the majority of revenue.

**Free Tier**
Build your audience with free content before introducing paid options. Convert readers when they're ready.

**Flexible Access Control**
Choose who sees what:
- Public posts (everyone)
- Subscriber-only posts (free + paid)
- Paid-only posts (premium content)

**Direct Stripe Integration**
Seamless payment processing powered by Stripe. Subscribers pay you directly—no middleman taking a cut of your earnings. Supports both one-time payments and recurring subscriptions with automatic invoicing and payment retry logic.

### Comments & Community

**Threaded Discussions**
Foster real conversations with threaded, nested replies. Readers can engage with each other, not just with you.

**Real-Time Updates**
Comments appear instantly without page refresh. Built with Hotwire and Turbo for a modern, responsive experience.

**Comment Voting**
Let your community surface the best comments. Upvotes help quality contributions rise to the top.

**Author Highlighting**
Your replies stand out in the discussion. Readers can easily find your responses and insights.

**Moderation Queue**
Review comments before they go live. Approve, reject, or mark as spam with a streamlined moderation workflow.

**Subscriber-Only Comments**
Reduce spam and increase quality by limiting comments to verified subscribers.

**Comment Notifications**
Keep the conversation going. Notify readers when someone replies to their comment or when you respond.

**Discussion Posts**
Create posts designed for conversation rather than broadcasting. Perfect for Q&As, community check-ins, or open discussions.

**Polls & Surveys**
Gather reader feedback directly in your posts. Single choice, multiple choice, ratings, or open-ended responses.

**Community Directory**
Help subscribers connect with each other. Optional profiles let readers discover others with shared interests.

**Chat & Discord Integration**
Link your publication to external community spaces. Integrate with Discord or Slack for real-time engagement.

### Analytics

**Post Performance**
Track views, unique readers, email opens, and clicks for every post.

**Engagement Scoring**

### SEO & Discovery

**Structured Data**
Schema.org markup for rich search results. Help Google understand and feature your content.

**Automatic Sitemaps**
Generated and updated automatically. Make sure search engines can find all your content.

**Social Cards**
Custom Open Graph and Twitter Card support. Control exactly how your posts appear when shared.

**Canonical URLs**
Proper handling for cross-posted content. Avoid duplicate content issues across platforms.

**Meta Descriptions**
Custom search snippets for every post. Improve click-through rates from search results.

**Noindex Controls**
Keep paywalled or private content out of search indexes when needed.

---

### Security

**Two-Factor Authentication**
Protect your account with TOTP-based 2FA. Support for Google Authenticator, Authy, and other authenticator apps.

**Audit Logs**
Complete history of account activity. Track logins, changes, and administrative actions.

**Rate Limiting**
Protection against abuse and brute-force attacks. Keep your publication secure without thinking about it.

**Magic Link Authentication**
Passwordless login for subscribers. Secure, simple, and friction-free.

## Technical Foundation

Prose is built on a modern, robust technology stack designed for reliability and performance.

### Stack

| Component | Technology |
|-----------|------------|
| Framework | Ruby on Rails 8.1 |
| Language | Ruby 4.0 |
| Database | SQLite |
| Caching | Solid Cache |
| Background Jobs | Solid Queue |
| Real-Time | Solid Cable + Hotwire/Turbo |
| Editor | Lexxy (Basecamp's Lexical-based editor) |
| Styling | Tailwind CSS |
| Email Service | Mailgun (Letter Opener in development) |
| Payments | Stripe |
| Authentication | Magic Links + 2FA |

### Design Principles

**Simplicity First**
A clean black, white, and grey color palette. No visual clutter—just your content.

**Performance**
Fast page loads, optimized images, and efficient database queries. Your readers shouldn't wait.

**Reliability**
SQLite for simplicity and durability. Solid Queue for reliable background processing. Built to run anywhere.

**Real-Time**
Hotwire and Turbo Streams power instant updates. Comments, votes, and notifications appear without page refresh.
